Mission

Doctor Diaries was founded over 8 years ago on a philosophy of education that believes students are best taught medicine in small classes, with one-on-one instruction, and with a commitment to providing an education on par with U.S. and Canadian medical schools.

Vision

Today Doctor Diaries continues to adhere to those founding principles, and has earned a reputation for academic excellence - and graduate success - that has made it a leader among international medical schools.
